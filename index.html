The NeuroSymbolic Reading Group

Welcome to the homepage of the NeuroSymbolic reading group at UT Austin. This group is broadly run by the members of the TRISHUL lab group which is led by Swarat Chaudhuri. The group is maintained by Josh Hoffman. We currently meet every Tuesday at 2pm CT in GDC and over zoom. Please reach out if you'd like the room number or zoom link.
