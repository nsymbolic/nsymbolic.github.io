The NeuroSymbolic Reading Group

Welcome to the homepage of the NeuroSymbolic reading group at UT Austin. This group is broadly run by the members of the TRISHUL lab group which is led by Swarat Chaudhuri and the AMRL lab group which is led by Joydeep Biswas. The group is maintained by Josh Hoffman. We currently meet every Tuesday at 2pm CT in GDC and over zoom. Please reach out if you'd like the room number or zoom link. Past and upcoming papers/topics are shared below. Enjoy!

NOTICE: In order to allow people to adjust to the new semester and due to upcoming deadlines, we will be putting the reading group on hold until October 1, 2022. We will resume our regular meetings then. Best of luck to everyone in their academic and research endeavors. Hook 'Em!
